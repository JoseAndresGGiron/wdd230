Target Audience

Our audience is all those business leaders who wish to accelerate the development of the community by developing and expanding their business networks.

At the same time, all members of the community who are looking for businesses to shop at or fundraise, are welcome to participate in this space.

  • Status: Single, Married or Other
  • Age: 20-60 approx
  • Education: Higher education
  • Most of user will be accessing the site through mobile devices such as smartphones or tablets.

Personas

Andres
He's 53 years old. Small business owner who in the last few years has been increasing his profits. Due to the pandemic, the development of his business has regressed, so he is looking for allies with whom he can associate so that together they can develop his business.

Scarlett
She's an investor looking for a local business to invest in. She wants to attend an event to connect with future candidates so she searches online for an upcoming event.

Scenarios

  • Is there a business I can partner with?
  • Is there an upcoming event where I can increase my business networks?
  • Is there a way I can advertise my business locally?
  • In which small local businesses can I buy to promote the development of my community?
  • Where can I start a fundraiser?
