PARC: Contrast

Amazon

Visit Amazon's site

Amazon is one of the sites I use the most for my online purchases. Amazon sells all kinds of products, and every product can be color different from each other, each product has a preview image about it. Can you imagine how hard is to contrast products of all kinds of colors? However, colors make a good contrast making the elements look different from each other while the site looks clean.

PARC: Alignment

Pandora

Visit Pandora's site

I enjoy the pandora website because everything is very well aligned. We can see the available products as a list. Everything looks very neat. There is a certain amount of space within each element. I have made some purchases through this website. Since it's very aligned purchasing has been so easy while searching for my product, comparing between different products, and finally, purchasing what I was looking for.
